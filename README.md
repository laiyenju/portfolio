## Featured Projects

### 1. FUBON Digital Experience (2024-2025)
**A Design System For Unified Customer Experience**

Established a scalable design foundation across the corporate group by implementing a unified design system, enabling consistent user experiences and efficient collaboration among 6 subsidiaries while elevating organizational design maturity.

- **Role:** Program Owner
- **Impact:** Unified customer experience across all subsidiaries, 20% improvement in development efficiency

### 2. Vibe Check in Action (2025)
**Evaluating AI Design Translation Capabilities**

A comparative assessment of AI design tools—Gemini 3 Pro, Claude Sonnet 4.5, and Figma Make Design—evaluating their ability to translate design concepts into functional interfaces across seven key dimensions.

- **Role:** Researcher
- **Focus:** AI design translation, Builder Mode workflows

### 3. Genie AI (2023-2024)
**AI Analytics SaaS for MarTech**

The platform integrates first-party and advertising data through its GA4 module, uses AI to automate manual analytics processes, and generates actionable insights that drive marketing decisions.

- **Role:** Product Manager/Owner
- **Tech Stack:** GA4 integration, AI automation, data analytics

### 4. Coke+ (2022)
**An App for Customer Loyalty Program**

Designed and prototyped a loyalty membership app incorporating all sub-brands under Coca-Cola for Hong Kong and Taiwan markets.

- **Role:** Design System, Information Architecture, Prototype
- **Goal:** 150,000 monthly active users

### 5. Family Mart (2021-2022)
**An Innovation Business Model for New Product**

Conducted user research and product testing to help FamilyMart define their target audience, validate the business model, and position their ready-to-eat product line in market.

- **Role:** Project Owner, User Research, Design Workshop
- **Deliverables:** User personas, value positioning, strategic recommendations

### 6. TR+ (2021)
**New Experience Design for E-Commerce**

Redesigned the e-commerce experience for Techforce Group's home living brands through CRM data analysis and strategic UX recommendations.

- **Role:** User Research, Strategy & Ideation
- **Focus:** Customer journey mapping, purchasing behavior analysis
